Overview

The BAI signal engine produces a composite 0–90 score (90% max is intentional — preventing false precision when data is partial). Scores ≥65 → BUY, 45–64 → HOLD, <45 → NO SIGNAL.

Layer 1 — Demand & Fundamentals (35%)

  • TSM Revenue YoY (30%): TSM fabs ~90% of leading-edge AI chips. YoY ≥30% → 100, 20–30% → 80, 10–20% → 50, 0–10% → 25, negative → 0.
  • NVDA Data Center (30%): Proxy for AI accelerator spend. Both QoQ and 4Q YoY positive + accelerating → 100, both positive → 70, one positive → 40, both negative → 0.
  • Hyperscaler Capex (25%): MSFT, GOOGL, AMZN, META, ORCL. 4–5/5 growing → 100, 3/5 → 70, 2/5 → 40, ≤1 → 0.
  • Earnings Surprise (15%): % of top 15 holdings beating EPS estimate. ≥70% → 100, 50–70% → 60, <50% → 20.

Layer 2 — Price Action & Breadth (30%)

  • Risk-Adjusted Momentum: BAI's 26-week Sharpe vs SPY's. Outperforms by ≥0.3 → 100, outperforms → 70, inline → 40, underperforms → 10.
  • Constituent Breadth: % of top 20 holdings above 50d MA. ≥75% → 100, 60–75% → 70, 40–60% → 40, <40% → 0.
  • New Highs vs Lows: Top 20 holdings, last 20 trading days. ≥5 highs + 0 lows → 100, more highs → 60, more lows → 0.

Layer 3 — Macro & Risk Regime (25%)

  • VIX Term Structure (40%): VIX/VIX3M ratio. <0.90 → 100, 0.90–1.00 → 60, 1.00–1.10 → 30, >1.10 → 0.
  • SOXX Trend (30%): Above 50d + 200d + golden cross → 100, above both → 70, one → 40, below both → 0.
  • Credit Conditions (30%): HYG/LQD 50d MA trend. Rising → 100, flat → 60, falling → 0.

Layer 4 — Don't Chase (Veto)

  • MA200 Extension: BAI >25% above 200d MA → cap score at 60.
  • RSI(14) > 80: BAI daily RSI above 80 → cap score at 60.

Additional Veto Rules

Three vetos cap the score at 60: (1) Macro layer = 0 (all indicators risk-off), (2) Don't-chase conditions above, (3) Any layer with >50% data fetch failures.

Limitations

  • yfinance data may be delayed, incomplete, or occasionally incorrect.
  • Holdings file requires quarterly manual refresh from blackrock.com.
  • BAI has limited trading history — backtesting is not yet reliable.
  • Decision-support tool only, not a standalone trading system.
